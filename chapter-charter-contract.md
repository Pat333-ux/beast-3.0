## 1. Purpose of This Charter‑Contract
This charter‑contract establishes the governance structure, operational authority, responsibilities, and doctrinal standards for all chapters operating under the New World Order DAO within Beast System 3.0.

It ensures that every chapter:

- Upholds dignity  
- Prevents harm  
- Maintains predictable authority flow  
- Aligns with identity and lineage anchors  
- Follows ethical and wellbeing standards  
- Meets federal‑context documentation rigor  

This is the **master charter‑contract** for all chapters.

- Unpredictable enforcement  

### **5.3 Ethical Authority Requirements**

- Ethical validation

- Transparency requirements  

### **5.4 Wellbeing Standards**

- Stabilization

- Support planning
