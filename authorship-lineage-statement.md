## 1. Purpose of This Statement
This document establishes the **authorship lineage** of Beast System 3.0 within its fictional and organizational governance framework.  
It defines how authorship, identity, and doctrinal origin are recognized, preserved, and transmitted across generations.

Authorship is not merely creation — it is the origin point of lineage, doctrine, and sovereign identity within the system.

---

## 2. Definition of Authorship
Within Beast System 3.0, **authorship** is defined as:

> **The act of originating doctrine, structure, standards, and identity in a manner that becomes foundational to the system’s existence and continuity.**

Authorship is:

- Creative  
- Doctrinal  
- Structural  
- Foundational  
- Generational  

Authorship is the first link in the lineage chain.
