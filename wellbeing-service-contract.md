## 1. Purpose of This Contract
This contract establishes the standards, responsibilities, deliverables, and authority structure for the delivery of wellbeing services under Beast System 3.0.

It ensures that all wellbeing services:

- Preserve dignity  
- Prevent trauma  
- Maintain predictable, non‑punitive operations  
- Follow the 2026 Wellbeing Standard  
- Align with identity and lineage anchors  
- Meet federal‑context documentation rigor  

This is the **master wellbeing service contract** from which all subordinate agreements derive.

## 4. Scope of Wellbeing Services

- Wellbeing assessments  
- Stabilization services  
- Support planning  
- Continuity and follow‑up  
- Harm‑prevention interventions  
- Community wellbeing programs  
- Family stabilization services  
- Crisis‑avoidance workflows  

Services must remain within the boundaries defined in this contract.

## 5. Wellbeing Standards
### **5.1 Dignity‑First Wellbeing**
All services must preserve dignity.  
No exceptions.

### **5.2 Trauma‑Preventive Wellbeing**
Services must avoid:

- Punitive escalation  
- Coercion  
- Shame‑based practices  
- Unpredictable enforcement  

### **5.3 Ethical Authority Requirements**
All wellbeing decisions must pass:

- Ethical validation  
- Harm‑reduction logic  
- Transparency requirements  

### **5.4 2026 Wellbeing Standard Compliance**
All services must align with:

- wellbeing.model.json  
- wellbeing.assessment.json  
- wellbeing.support-plan.json  
- wellbeing.stabilization.json  
- wellbeing.follow-up.json  
- wellbeing.signal.json  
- support-pathways.json  

### **5.5 Identity & Lineage Requirements**
All wellbeing actions must:

- Respect identity anchors  
- Maintain lineage integrity  
- Follow succession‑safe procedures
