## 1. Purpose of This Contract
This contract establishes the operational framework, authority structure, responsibilities, and procedural standards for all activities conducted under the New World Order DAO within Beast System 3.0.

It ensures that all operations:

- Uphold dignity  
- Prevent harm  
- Maintain predictable authority flow  
- Follow ethical and wellbeing standards  
- Align with lineage and identity anchors  
- Meet federal‑context documentation rigor  

This is the **master operational contract** from which all subordinate operational agreements derive.
