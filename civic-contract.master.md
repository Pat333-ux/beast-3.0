## 1. Purpose of This Master Contract
This master framework defines the structure, standards, and doctrinal requirements for all civic contracts executed within Beast System 3.0.  
It ensures that every agreement:

- Upholds dignity  
- Reduces harm  
- Aligns with trauma‑preventive governance  
- Respects identity and lineage anchors  
- Maintains predictable authority flow  
- Meets federal‑context documentation standards  

This template is the parent document for all derivative civic contracts.

## 5. Governance Standards
All civic contracts must comply with the following doctrinal standards:

### **5.1 Dignity‑First Governance**
No action may violate dignity invariants.

### **5.2 Trauma‑Preventive Governance**
All procedures must reduce harm and avoid punitive escalation.

### **5.3 Ethical Authority Framework**
Authority must be predictable, transparent, and non‑coercive.

### **5.4 Wellbeing Standards**
All services must align with stabilization, assessment, support planning, continuity, and harm‑prevention domains.

### **5.5 Lineage & Succession**
Authority must remain traceable and succession‑safe.
